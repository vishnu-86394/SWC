How Collection Works

1) Segregate

Keep wet, dry, and reject waste separate at source. Use labeled bins.

2) Set‑out by 7 AM

Place bins at curb/collection point before the vehicle arrives.

3) Smart Tracking

Set‑out Guidelines

  • Rinse bottles & cans. Flatten cardboard.
  • Bag sanitary waste separately in red bags.
  • No leaking liquids or hazardous chemicals.
  • Bundle e‑waste and label with device types.
  • Keep pets away during collection window.

FAQ

Do I need to compost at home?

Not necessary. We compost organic waste at our facility. Home composting is encouraged but optional.

What if my bin is damaged?

Raise a ticket via the form with photos. We repair/replace within 72 hours for subscribed users.

Can businesses schedule night pickups?

Yes. Night windows (9–11 PM) are available for commercial streets. Mention in the notes field.

Do you collect hazardous waste?

No. Contact authorized facilities for chemicals, paints, or biomedical waste.
