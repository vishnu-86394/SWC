Smart Waste Rewards & Tips

Rewards

Earn Green Points for each correctly segregated bin.
Redeem points for shopping vouchers and utility bill discounts.
Top households get recognition badges in the community leaderboard.

Tips

  • Rinse plastic bottles before putting them in the dry bin.
  • Crush cans and flatten cardboard to save bin space.
  • Store e-waste separately and request a pickup when full.
  • Compost kitchen waste at home to earn bonus points.
